your union

Our mission

Welcome to UCLan Students’ Union where it’s our job to make life better for students and that’s why, in everything we do, we work with you to make your experience at UCLan the best it can possibly be.

When you enrol onto a UCLan course, you’re automatically a member, it’s free and everyone’s included. The best part of a Students’ Union is that it’s led by students elected by you, our members! From the get-go you’re one of us and whether it’s advice, getting involved or joining us for a coffee or a beer we’re here for you.

Tell us what’s important to you so that together, we can campaign to change things on campus, on your course and in the community. There’s tonnes of opportunities where you can join a club or society, volunteering project or represent students on your course. All these and more will boost your skills and help you make friends.
