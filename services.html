Services

Eat, Drink, Shop

We have it all at the Union. Enjoy an evening drink or an afternoon bite to eat at our award winning Source bar or grab a Starbucks and chat away in The Atrium. Pop down to our Union shop and grab a meal deal or a quick refreshment to fuel your studies. Get kitted out in UCLan Merch from our FACTORY clothing line. We're a not-for-profit organisation which means every penny you spend in the Union, goes straight back into what we do for you.

Every year, we recuit student staff who support us in running our services. If you're looking for part-time work, alongside your studies, in a flexible, dynamic and fast-paced organisation then keep checking our website for our job opportunities.
