Top tips

Be informed

What is Freshers?

Freshers week is an exciting time for all University Students and is an amazing opportunity time to meet new people. There is something for everybody, whether you like partying or getting involved in day time activities. Keep up to date by visiting uclansu.co.uk or by downloading the TOTUM app.

How can the Students’ Union support me?

Through independent Union advice and effective awareness campaigns the Union will support Students to make independent and informed decisions. We will provide opportunities to join and create Societies and Sports clubs that will help you best connect with your friends.

We work closely with the University to ensure that Students and their families are prepared for and are aware of all the support available to aid transition to University. Please visitour support pages.

What is TOTUM?

TOTUM is the number one UK student discount card - and now app! It is the new name for the NUS extra student discount card and your TOTUM membership opens up a world of fantastic student discounts, offers and vouchers on your everyday essentials, must-have gear and luxury items.

How do I join a Sports club and Society?

We have a wide variety of sports and societies. To view what we have available, do visit our sports and society’s pages.You can sign up to a sport or society from 1st August 2019. Once you arrive come along to our Societies Fair on 17th September and Sports Fair on 18th September where you will be able to speak to members, see demos and sign up.

If you don’t make it, don’t worry. Most clubs and societies are open all year round.

How can I stay keep up to date with what’s going on in the Students’ Union?

First things first, sign up to our site with your @uclan.ac.uk email account. Here we offer up to date information and provide a platform to sign up to Sports clubs and Societies.

Follow our Social channels: Facebook,Instagram, Twitter.
