volunteer

get experience!

Our student-led Inspiring Projects are a way for you to develop your skills, give back to the community and meet new people. If you’re short of time for regular volunteering, get involved with our One-off Wednesday opportunities which run every week of term time. Or if you have an interest in media, our on-campus student media platform Pulse could be for you.

No experience necessary as we’ll provide all the training you need! We reward our volunteers with prizes and certificates as part of the Livesey Award – so they look great on your CV.

Contact suvolunteering@uclan.ac.uk for more information or check out the link below to get involved."
